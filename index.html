Nishimura riki

Birth date: 9 December 2005

Birth place: Okayama, Japan

NI-KI (니키) is a Japanese singer and rapper under Belift Lab. He is the maknae of the boy group ENHYPEN.

On June 4, 2020, NI-KI was introduced as a contestant in the CJ ENM and Big Hit Entertainment's reality survival show I-LAND. On September 18, 2020, he ranked 4th place by global votes and became a member of the boy group ENHYPEN.
