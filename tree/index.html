Your Name

(Japanese: 君の名は。, Hepburn: Kimi no Na wa.)

Your Name is a 2016 Japanese animated romantic fantasy film produced by CoMix Wave Films and released by Toho. It depicts a high school boy in Tokyo and a high school girl in the Japanese countryside who suddenly and inexplicably begin to swap bodies.

Weathering With You

(Japanese: 天気の子, Hepburn: Tenki no Ko, lit. "Child of Weather")

Weathering with You is a 2019 Japanese animated romantic fantasy film produced by CoMix Wave Films and released by Toho. It depicts a high school boy who runs away from his rural home to Tokyo and befriends an orphan girl who has the ability to control the weather.

One piece

(Japanese: ワンピース, Hepburn: Wan Pīsu.)

One Piece is story follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy, a boy whose body gained the properties of rubber after eating a Devil Fruit. With his crew of pirates, Luffy explores the Grand Line in search of the world's treasure known as "One Piece" and become the next King of the Pirates.
